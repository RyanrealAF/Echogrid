# **App Name**: StemSculpt Visualizer

## Core Features:

- Synchronized Playback & Lyric Display: Stream individual song stems and synchronize lyric display, highlighting active words based on precise timing data.
- Dynamic Waveform Visualization: Render dynamic waveform data for each stem, pulsing the main waveform and specific stems in sync with the audio beats and production cues.
- Contextual Visual Effects Engine: Apply real-time visual treatments and animations (e.g., color shifts, opacity changes, glows, wobbles) to lyrics, waveforms, and background elements based on song section metadata (type, speaker, production cues, accents).
- File-based Metadata & Audio Streaming: Manage and serve song metadata (lyrics, beats, waveform data) from JSON files and stream individual audio stem WAV files from a designated backend structure.
- Section Navigator & Control Panel: Allow users to navigate through song sections and control playback (play/pause).
- AI-Enhanced Visual Treatment Tool: Utilize AI as a tool to suggest creative textual descriptions or supplementary visual prompts for a given song section's 'production' cues (e.g., 'vinylcrackle', 'helicopterdistant'), enriching the overall visual narrative.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color palette is inspired by 'cinematic grime' and 'glowing neon within shadows', aiming for a moody yet dynamic aesthetic suitable for 'LOVE IN THE RUIN'.
- Background color: Deep Indigo (#1A161E), heavily desaturated and dark, providing a strong contrast for illuminated elements.
- Primary accent color: Luminous Violet (#774ADD), used for highlighted lyrics, active section indicators, and pulsing waveform glows. This shade is vibrant enough to stand out against the dark background.
- Secondary accent color: Soft Cyan-Blue (#8CCBFF), analogous to the primary and distinctly lighter, offering contrast for secondary glows, subtle effects, and interactive elements.
- Headline and emphasized text font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), for a modern, rhythmic, and impactful feel, especially for glowing words and section labels.
- Body and general text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its excellent readability and neutral appearance, ensuring synchronized lyrics are clear and unobtrusive.
- Minimalist line icons with subtle glowing effects for playback controls and interactive elements, matching the futuristic yet gritty visual theme.
- An immersive, full-screen layout that dynamically reconfigures to prioritize current lyrical content and waveform visualizations, adapting space and composition based on section 'type' and 'production' cues.
- Subtle background oscillations, word scaling, and color shifts to reflect 'production' cues and 'speaker' changes. Glowing effects will pulse rhythmically with beats, and words with 'accent: true' will notably glow and scale up.
